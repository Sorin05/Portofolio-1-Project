A little background Info!

Irish Award Winners!

Artisan Bakery opened its doors in February 2018, with a modest selection of pastries and coffees. Since then we have added our own sourdough breads and more pastries to our menu. In August 2020 we won “Bakery of the year” in the National Bakery Awards.

We care!

We care about our customers and our food. We believe that quality ingredients make quality products. We use Irish flour from Kells for all our pastries and breads, Irish butter from Avonmore.
